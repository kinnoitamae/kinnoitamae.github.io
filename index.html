IDEAL MEAL is a field game about teamwork and doing crazy things to make the perfect bowl of ramen! Armed only with a set of oversized chopsticks, work with your fellow chefs to to transport giant ingredients into a humongous ramen bowl while meeting whatever the recipe calls for along the way.


Some of these instructions may be wacky—spinning in circles, hopping on one foot, and even running with your eyes closed—but no matter what, you and your team HAVE to follow the recipe. Making the Ideal Meal isn’t easy, and it’ll take everyone’s cooking skills to do it!
